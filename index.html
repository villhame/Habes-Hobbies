This is a Habes hobbies fan website

Welcome to Habes Hobbies, your one-stop channel for all things 3D printed Warhammer miniatures. The HH channel is dedicated to showcasing the latest and greatest in the world of tabletop gaming, with a special focus on the intricate and beloved miniatures of the Warhammer universe. Here you'll find everything from painting tutorials and unboxings of 3D printed minis, to battle reports and strategy guides using these minis. Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the hobby, we have something for everyone. Join us as we explore the exciting world of 3D printing and Warhammer minis, and discover new ways to enhance your gaming experience. Don't forget to subscribe to our channel to stay up-to-date with our latest content and hit the notification bell to never miss a video.
